My Story

I was born and raised in Cleveland, OH. While my wife and I spent some time on the west coast, we couldn't be happier about being back in Cleveland. My bachelor's is in English and my master's is in writing. While working as a copywriter for a search engine optimization company in CA, I realized I was fascinated by code.

Upon returning to Cleveland, I enrolled in We Can Code It. I am now a full-stack junior developer, skilled in C#, HTML, CSS, SQL, JavaScript, and ASP.NET MVC. I enjoy hiking and spending time with my wife.
